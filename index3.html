This is a scream.


It wraps around your throat,


contracts your lungs and


binds your legs together.


Oddly enough it is a bandage dress,


and people say it is quite slimming on you.

There is a sweater, knitted of 3 am's.


Some of the stripes are late nights, working.


Some are ticking clocks staring at your ceiling.


Some are sleepy bliss, the walk home with your best friend.


(It is suggested that you wear this to bed)

This is a wrap made of both water and ocean waves,


it ebbs and flows as it to rock you to sleep.
